## Summary
A **concept page** describes a pattern, strategy, technique, or architectural decision. It answers the question *"how is X done?"* in three to five dense lines. The Summary block is the single most important section because it is what other pages cite and what the index summary derives from.

## Why It Exists
State the problem the concept solves. One paragraph. If the answer is "for completeness" or "because it seemed nice", the concept does not warrant its own page.

## How It Works
The mechanics. Use tables, code blocks, or numbered steps. Be specific about file paths, function names, and constraints. Avoid prose paragraphs longer than five lines.

## Trade-offs
Honest about cost. What does this pattern make harder? What does it preclude? What alternatives were considered and why were they rejected?
